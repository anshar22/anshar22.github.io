Wildlife & Forest Cover Assessment

Conducted ecological field surveys in Solok Pesisir to assess wildlife presence and forest conditions. Aquatic Sampling & Pollution Monitoring

Assisted in the collection of plankton and benthos samples as part of environmental monitoring for industrial wastewater impact. Familiar with sampling equipment and procedures, contributing to the assessment of aquatic ecosystem health and regulatory compliance.

Cave Biodiversity Exploration

Participated in cave expeditions to collect bat samples and identify reptile and amphibian species. This initiative supported biodiversity documentation and enhanced understanding of cave-dwelling fauna for scientific and educational purposes.

Zoonotic Disease Ecology Study

Contributed to a joint research project on virus transmission in long-tailed and pig-tailed macaques. Focused on observing environmental conditions, habitat characteristics, and behavioral patterns to support analysis of disease vectors and wildlife-human interactions.

Community Based Fish Habitat Conservation

Secured a small grant to implement a habitat conservation initiative using rumpons (Fish Aggregating Devices) to support fish population recovery. Led planning and installation efforts, engaging local stakeholders to enhance sustainable fisheries and protect aquatic biodiversity.

Biodiversity conservation in the KEHATI Emil Salim Park

Conducted mixed methods research to analyze local community perceptions and behaviors toward biodiversity conservation in the KEHATI Emil Salim Park. Designed and administered surveys residents, facilitated focus group discussions with key stakeholders, and mapped community ecosystem interaction patterns.

Wildlife Ecology Assessment Way Kambas Conservation Area, Sumatra

I volunteered to help study the eating habits of endangered elephants and rhinos in Way Kambas. Working with international researchers, I gathered important data about what plants the animals eat and when they feed. This information is now being used to protect and restore their forest habitats.
